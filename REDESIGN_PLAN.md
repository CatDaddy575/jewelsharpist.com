# Jewels Harpist Website Redesign Plan

## Current Site Audit

### What's Working
- Basic structure (navigation, services, contact form)
- Mobile menu exists
- Serves core purpose (info + contact)

### What Needs Improvement

#### Design & Appeal
- Dated/generic look — doesn't convey elegance of a professional harpist
- Limited imagery/content
- No portfolio or gallery of performances
- No testimonials or social proof
- Limited "About Julia" — credentials, experience, bio missing
- No pricing/packages visible

#### SEO & Discoverability
- Likely weak SEO (no structured data, limited keyword content, etc.)
- No blog or rich content
- No video (critical for musicians — people want to hear/see performances)
- Probably not ranking for competitive search terms
- Poor content depth

#### Functionality
- Contact form exists but no confirmation or follow-up automation
- No online booking system (just "contact me")
- No way to preview prices or packages before contacting
- No way to share social proof or reviews

#### Technical
- Unknown performance metrics
- Mobile experience needs verification
- No analytics tracking visible

## Redesign Goals

1. **Visual Appeal** — modern, elegant design that matches a professional musician
2. **Content Rich** — portfolio, testimonials, detailed services, about section
3. **SEO Optimized** — proper keywords, structure, content depth, speed
4. **Functional** — better booking flow, pricing clarity, social proof
5. **Fast & Accessible** — mobile-first, good performance, accessible design

## What We'll Build

### Tech Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js or Static HTML/CSS/JS (decide based on your needs)
- **Hosting**: Vercel or Netlify (free tier)
- **Domain**: Keep jewelsharpist.com

### Pages & Sections
1. **Home** 
   - Hero section with high-quality imagery
   - Services overview
   - Testimonials/social proof
   - CTA to book

2. **About** 
   - Julia's bio, credentials, experience
   - Professional photo
   - Music philosophy

3. **Services** 
   - Detailed service descriptions (Corporate, Weddings, Funerals, etc.)
   - Pricing/packages
   - What's included

4. **Portfolio** 
   - Gallery of past events
   - Videos of performances (if available)
   - Case studies/testimonials

5. **Contact/Book** 
   - Contact form with better UX
   - Calendar integration for availability
   - Clear next steps

6. **Footer** 
   - Location, hours, contact info
   - Links to social media (if any)

### SEO Improvements
- Proper meta tags and descriptions
- Structured data (Schema.org for local business, services)
- Keyword optimization
- Fast page load
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Sitemap and robots.txt
